Simone Caputo is an architectural designer and 3D artist based in Italy. Blending the rigorous methodology of architectural design with the boundless possibilities of digital fabrication and VFX, his work creates a seamless bridge between the physical and the virtual.

With a strong foundation in spatial analysis and morphology, Simone specializes in motion graphics, CGI integration, and Fake Out Of Home (FOOH) campaigns, crafting visual narratives that are both structurally sound and visually striking.
